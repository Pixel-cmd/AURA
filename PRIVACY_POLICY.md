# Privacy Policy for AURA

**Last Updated:** December 30, 2025

## Introduction

AURA ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- We collect your location data only when you activate SOS or enable Helper Mode
- Location is shared only with nearby helpers during an active SOS request
- We do not track your location continuously or store location history
- Location data is used solely for matching you with nearby helpers

### Personal Information
- Phone number (for authentication)
- First name (displayed to helpers)
- Device information (for security)

### Usage Data
- SOS request history (for rate limiting and safety)
- Helper responses (for reputation system)
- Points and badges (for gamification)

## How We Use Your Information

- To connect you with nearby helpers during emergencies
- To verify your identity and prevent abuse
- To calculate reputation scores and award points
- To send push notifications for SOS alerts
- To improve app functionality and safety

## Data Sharing

- **With Helpers:** Only first name and approximate distance (not exact location)
- **With Emergency Services:** No automatic sharing. You must manually call emergency services.
- **Third Parties:** We do not sell or share your data with third parties

## Data Security

- All data is encrypted in transit
- Firestore database with security rules
- User authentication required
- Rate limiting to prevent abuse

## Your Rights

- Access your data
- Delete your account
- Report abuse or inappropriate behavior
- Block other users

## No Guarantee of Help

**IMPORTANT:** AURA does not guarantee that help will be available or that helpers will respond. In case of immediate danger, always call your local emergency number (112, 911, etc.).
